## ğŸ® Game Features

-   **3 Progressive Levels**: Each level increases in difficulty with unique challenges.
-   **Double Jump Mechanics**: Perform up to two jumps for enhanced platforming.
-   **Collectibles**: Gather coins and cherries to increase your score.
-   **Enemy Combat**: Jump on mushrooms from above to defeat them.
-   **Hazards**: Avoid fire that costs you a life upon contact.
-   **Lives System**: Start with 3 lives; lose one when damaged or touching fire.
-   **Smooth Controls**: Responsive movement for a fluid gameplay experience.
-   **Sound Effects**: Immersive audio for actions like collecting items and defeating enemies.
-   **Pause Functionality**: Pause the game at any time.

## ğŸ¯ Gameplay Mechanics

-   **Player Character**: Runs automatically, with animations for idle, running, and jumping. The sprite flips based on movement direction and creates particle effects on double jumps.
-   **Enemies (Mushrooms)**: Can be defeated by jumping on them from above. They will push the player back and deal damage if touched from the side or below.
-   **Collectibles**: Coins and cherries are scattered throughout the levels to increase your score.
-   **Hazards**: Watch out for fire pits that reduce a life and falls that restart the level.
-   **Level Progression**: Complete levels by reaching the finish flag to progress from Level 1 to 3 and see the final congratulations screen.
